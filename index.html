About

About me

I'm a full stack developer based in Hobart, Tasmania. CS degree from North South University (Dhaka) and a Masters in IT from Flinders University (2023–2025).

What drives me is the full picture — hardware talking to a server, a server powering a web app, a web app syncing with a mobile app. Distributed systems where every layer connects is where I naturally want to be. I've shipped iOS and Android apps to production, built multi-tenant SaaS platforms, integrated payment systems, and wired up IoT pipelines from sensor to dashboard.

Coding has never been just a job for me. Through every difficult stretch — job searches, rejections, starting from scratch in a new country — I kept building projects. I can't not build. That consistency is the best thing I can say about myself as a developer.
